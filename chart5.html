Emissions by Day

This is a chart of daily emissions for the contiguous United States. There is a clear drop every Saturday and Sunday. Zoom (mouse wheel) and pan (drag) the chart below to explore the data. Hovering over a bar will show that day's total emissions in a tooltip above the bar.

Use the three buttons below to isolate weekdays or weekend days only. Some days have totals that seem to break the weekly pattern. Can you figure out why?
